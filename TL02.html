Emotions

I'm rather busy laughing and crying,
practically feeling like being swept away in a giant mass
This disorderliness in my head has been killing me
"Deep breaths..." Even breathing is agonizing

The hand that covered you as you exhaled
is a hand sign that reads, "It's all over."
You'll let yourself be stolen just 'cause you're sad
that we, who wish to meet, cannot meet

You are alone, lost forever
All it takes is one of those shards to cut your finger
So don't you lose the wonderful things,
embrace these tender lumps in your arms

Those awful sobs that leave you gasping for breath,
suits you so well for your normalcy.
There can be no mistake, nor is there time to change
I was too deceptive for my own good

Your two rightly inexperienced arms
are clinging to me, who is not enough
Back off, before you wish for it,
before you get a taste of a world that won't be shining.

Before you dream

You've hit rock bottom, don't try to prove yourself
It has to be me, it can't be me either
The aftertaste is bad, thus it tastes good
I can't forgive you 'cause you're so dazzling

Look at us, the two who fall in unison
Décolleté without a mark, we're closing the distance
You even brought up something unnecessary,
to overwrite scars that do not heal

Neither too shallow wits nor too sweet a face
it is right there and you want to feel it
Neither to be your leader nor your follower
I love you in lieu of something else

I'm letting you say things
that doesn't make any sense aloud
Children are scolded, cats are killed
This passion of yours will crumble here

Going into the sky but unable to go
Flying into the sky, but unable to fly
It'll feel like eternity, even if I withdraw
it's going to keep hurting.

Your two rightly inexperienced arms
are binding me, who is not enough
Don't be so ashamed, red eye
Don't be so timid, little one

Those awful sobs that leave you gasping for breath,
suits you so well for your normalcy.
There cannot be a mistake, nor is there time to change
My sheer deception made you wave the white flag

Ahh

The body itself is stained and vanishes
To what extent do I own you now?
These hands that judge you so blatantly
I still mistake it for something beautiful

キドアイラク (喜怒哀楽): ＊　Kidoairaku, the title of the song, is a Yojijukugo. The meaning of it is human emotions (joy, anger, grief and pleasure).
