Still Alive

This was a triumph... I'm making a note here, Huge Success.

I thought I was going to die. Maybe that is a crazy way to start off the year. Hi... my medication did not interact well with caffeine this time.

No matter how many times situations like these happen, you can never really get truly used to it unless if you're placed in a battle arena. It's an experience unlike another but that is why I find it valuable - it reminds me that I am alive. I value these experiences no matter how painful it is physically.

Some people believe they will approach these situations calmly, or will even accept it when the time comes, but you never truly know until you get put into a similar situation. It might be weird to say, but it's exhilarating.
