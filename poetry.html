Untitled

2021

nothingness

2025

What happens when the fire is gone
but no one tells you how to reignite it?

Emotions were just distractions,
noise in the void,
though, now, the void is all that is left.

I reach for something,
but there is nothing to hold.
Perhaps that's the point.

Perhaps this is it -
no fire, no spark,
just the cold, empty space
where feeling used to be.

Stain

2022

You are like a fly in the ointment,
one small event that spoils the whole day.

Yes, we have trouble leaving each other alone;
but it's not attraction.
It is a need to be held, understood, and listened to.

Like a stain on a windowpane
that has been hastily washed away,
in order to preserve the good parts of the glass.

In spite of our similarities,
I tremble in your presence,
as if death was greeting me on my doorstep.

Like the final arc of a story,
the one person - the one event,
I must not approach
if I don't want the narrative to end.

Staring at the clock, waiting for time to pass.
For you to pass.
To leave me alone.

I will not let you in;
I will not let you touch;
I will not let you love yourself through me.
I don't want to be your anchor; your savior.

You continuously make new stains on the windowpane,
forcing me to clean it every time,
reminding me of your existence.
"Let me in", your lips enunciate.

If this person is let in,
they'll force me to stay with them.
They'll force me to call this unfamiliar place "home".

So, instead of staying in this place any longer,
I will instead disappear.

That way, you'll stop visiting me.

untitled

2021

"Why can't I love?
"Why can't I care?
Why can't I cherish?"

I often had
thoughts like that.

Then I had realized - I do care. I think so.
Far more than I could've ever known, I've realized that I do have emotions.
I can become hurt by people, but I can't be hurt by what I do.
I can become sad because of myself, but I cannot care about most of other people's sorrows, including their suicidal ideations.
I can be as distant as I've always been, but there had been times where I desired for a connection with a human being. A connection where I won't have to filter myself.

I realized that no matter how irredeemable this kind of being that I am, I've managed to come to know that a person is deserving of attempting to do better. No matter who I was or what I had done.

It came to me like that; fleeting but the feeling was certainly there at some point. That change isn't restricted to people who are defined as "good" in this world.

Change doesn't choose people upon morality, but rather the person themselves.

It's when I've come to see that nothing really mattered in the end.
