This is the poetry page, the expressing point.

nothing

2025

What happens
when the fire is gone
but no one tells you
how to reignite it?

Emotions were just distractions,
noise in the void,
and now the void’s all that’s left.

I reach for something,
but there’s nothing to hold.
Maybe that's the point.

Maybe this is it-
no fire,
no spark,
just the cold, empty space
where feeling used to be.

sanctuary.
