Stain

2022

You are like a fly in the ointment, one small event that spoils the whole day.

Yes, we have trouble leaving each other alone; but it's not attraction. It is a need to be held, understood, and listened to.

Like a stain on a windowpane that has been hastily washed away, in order to preserve the good parts of the glass.

In spite of our similarities, I tremble in your presence, as if death was greeting me on my doorstep.

Like the final arc of a story, the one person - the one event, I must not approach if I don't want the narrative to end.

Staring at the clock, waiting for time to pass. For you to pass. To leave me alone.

I will not let you in; I will not let you touch; I will not let you love yourself through me. I don't want to be your anchor; your savior.

You continuously make new stains on the windowpane, forcing me to clean it every time, reminding me of your existence. "Let me in", your lips enunciate.

If this person is let in, they'll force me to stay with them. They'll force me to call this unfamiliar place "home".

So, instead of staying in this place any longer, I will instead; disappear.

That way, you'll stop visiting me.

nothing

2025

What happens
when the fire is gone
but no one tells you
how to reignite it?

Emotions were just distractions,
noise in the void,
and now the void’s all that’s left.

I reach for something,
but there’s nothing to hold.
Maybe that's the point.

Maybe this is it-
no fire,
no spark,
just the cold, empty space
where feeling used to be.
